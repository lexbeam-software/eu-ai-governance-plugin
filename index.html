Capabilities

Six Governance Commands

Each command is a structured, multi-step workflow with regulatory citations, evidence checklists, and actionable outputs.

⚖
/classify-ai-risk
EU AI Act risk classification
▾

8-step gated classification: prohibited practices (Art. 5), Annex I safety components, Annex III high-risk use cases, GPAI model obligations, and limited-risk transparency duties. Produces a regulator-ready assessment with obligations, evidence checklist, and 30/60/90-day action plan.

Sample output

> /classify-ai-risk "TalentScreen AI - automated resume screening" Classification: HIGH-RISK (Annex III) Category: Employment, workers management (Cat. 4a) Role: Deployer Key obligations: Art. 26 deployer duties, Art. 27 FRIA, Works Council consultation (BetrVG §87(1) No. 6) Evidence gaps: 4 items requiring vendor documentation Next steps: 8 actions across 30/60/90 days
⚙
/assess-ai-vendor
Vendor contract assessment
▾

Three-dimensional assessment of AI vendor relationships: EU AI Act deployer obligations (Art. 26), GDPR and DPA requirements, enterprise governance and commercial risk. Produces GREEN/YELLOW/RED flags with contract redline suggestions and fallback positions.

Sample output

> /assess-ai-vendor "ChatGPT Enterprise - DPA and Terms" Overall risk: Medium Recommendation: Proceed with conditions Key findings: 1. Training data opt-out, RED, not contractually guaranteed 2. Sub-processor changes, YELLOW, no objection right 3. AI Act documentation, YELLOW, not provided Proposed redlines: 8 contract amendments (3 P1, 3 P2, 2 P3)
⚠
/run-dpia
AI-tailored impact assessment
▾

Full DPIA combining GDPR Art. 35 with AI Act-aligned extensions: training data assessment, transparency and explainability evaluation, automated decision-making (Art. 22), fundamental rights considerations, and feedback loop analysis. Ready for DPO review.

Sample output

> /run-dpia "ChurnGuard - customer churn prediction model" Risk rating (pre-mitigation): Medium Residual risk: Low-Medium Identified risks: 7 (2 High, 3 Medium, 2 Low) R5: Lack of transparency, HIGH R6: No profiling opt-out, HIGH Mitigations: 5 technical + 5 organizational measures Decision: Proceed with conditions (Q1 2026 measures)
■
/ai-act-status
Compliance posture assessment
▾

Structured organizational assessment by enforcement deadline tier. Screens for prohibited practice risks, evaluates GPAI readiness, and benchmarks high-risk system preparedness. Produces a gap analysis with prioritized action plan.

Sample output

> /ai-act-status Overall posture: PARTIALLY COMPLIANT Tier A (Feb 2025): Compliant, no prohibited practices Tier B (Aug 2025): Partial, GPAI documentation gaps Tier C (Aug 2026): Non-compliant, 4 critical gaps Tier D (Aug 2027): Not applicable Urgent actions: 3 | Quick wins: 5 | Major projects: 2
☰
/generate-evidence-pack
Auditor-ready documentation
▾

Compiles governance documentation into a structured evidence pack with 10 sections: AI inventory, compliance status, DPIA summaries, vendor assessments, training records, incident log, policies, human oversight, change log, and gap analysis. Three output formats available.

Sample output

> /generate-evidence-pack --format document --lang en Evidence Pack compiled: 10 sections Systems covered: 8 AI systems across 3 departments Evidence indexed: 23 artifacts Gaps identified: 3 (2 medium priority, 1 low) Sections: Inventory | Compliance Status | DPIAs Vendor Assessments | Training | Incidents Policies | Human Oversight | Change Log | Gaps
✎
/review-ai-policy
Policy review and drafting
▾

Reviews existing AI governance policies or drafts new ones from scratch. Assesses 13+ required sections (governance structure, lifecycle, risk classification, human oversight, transparency, data governance, bias, incidents, vendor management) with GREEN/YELLOW/RED ratings.

Sample output

> /review-ai-policy "company-ai-policy-v2.pdf" Mode: Review Sections assessed: 13 Purpose and scope, GREEN Governance structure, GREEN Risk classification, YELLOW, missing Annex III mapping Human oversight, YELLOW, needs specific roles Incident management, RED, not addressed Top 3 gaps with suggested policy text provided
